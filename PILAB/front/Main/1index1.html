Dear friends and creators!

We are a small team of enthusiasts united by a passion for art and technology. PiLab was born from the dream of creating a space where every artist can freely share their works, get inspired, and connect.

We develop this website absolutely free of charge, without any hidden motives. Our goal is to make the world of art more accessible and vibrant. We don't ask for money because we believe that creativity should be free.

However, if our work touches your heart and you feel the desire to support us, we will be sincerely grateful. Any amount you decide to send will help us develop the platform further: add new features, improve the design, and support the community.

Your support is not only financial help, but also recognition of our work. Thank you for being with us!

With warmth, the PiLab team
